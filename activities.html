Activities at Pacific Trails

Pacific Trails Resort offers a variety of activities for guests to enjoy the beauty of the California North Coast.

  • Guided hiking tours along scenic coastal trails
  • Swimming in the heated outdoor pool and relaxing in the whirlpool
  • Kayaking and exploring the ocean
  • Evening campfires and social gatherings at the lodge
  • Fine dining with ocean views each night
